Design notes

  • Single input, parser does the work. Amount + free-text note typed together. The parser extracts the leading number as RSD and the rest as the note. No separate amount/note fields.
  • Real-time parsed readout. As soon as the input has a number, the readout card appears below. Confirms the parse before the user commits.
  • Top category auto-selected. Submit button reads "Add 350 RSD to Kafe i voda" — committing without re-tapping. Other suggestions are one-tap reassign.
  • Recent chips on empty state. The most-frequent / most-recent transactions become one-tap clones. This is what makes "log a daily coffee" 1 tap, not 3.
  • Success toast surfaces budget context. "2.140 / 4.000 this week · 1.860 left" — the user instantly knows where this entry leaves them. Undo for 10 seconds.
  • Optimistic insert. The new row appears in the feed immediately with the user's color highlight (novica blue here, distinct from the rose used for partner-just-synced).
