Design notes

  • Google is primary, email is equal-but-secondary. Google goes first because it's the lower-friction path; the "or" divider weights both options equally.
  • Single screen, three states. Toggling between sign-in and create-account doesn't navigate to a new route — it's the same form swapping the heading and button label. Keeps cognitive load low.
  • Focus state shows the design system. Inputs use a 2px brand border + 4px tinted ring (brand-100). The "currently focused" field on State 2 demonstrates this clearly.
  • Password strength in-line. 4 segments, color-coded. Removes the need for a separate "password requirements" toast.
  • Footer microcopy is the value prop. "One household, every device — synced live" telegraphs the multi-user, multi-device, realtime nature without an onboarding tour. No numerical claims about household size or device count.
