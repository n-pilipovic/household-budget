Design notes

  • Reached from Monthly. Tap a category row in the Monthly view → this screen. The breadcrumb (desktop) / back arrow (mobile) returns to the source month.
  • Single chart, no toggle. 12-month bar chart with variance-coded fills tells the whole story. Resist adding line charts, dual axes, or category-vs-category comparison — that's the v2 Reports view.
  • Filter chips, not a date picker. 90% of the time you want "all time" or "this month" or "last 30 days". A date picker is overkill for a personal app.
  • Quick Add stays in place. With this category as the pre-suggested one — it's the most likely category you'd be entering after looking at the trend.
  • "More" menu is dormant in MVP. The three-dot button is visible but its actions (rename, archive, change icon) ship in v1.1 with category management.
